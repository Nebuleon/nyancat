Nyan Cat! Nyanyanyanyanyanya!

- OpenDingux version, 2014-01-13 -

1. Nyanning
2. Un-nyanning
3. Source code and license

== Nyanning ==
Upon opening the application, you will begin to "nyan" along with a cat shaped like a Pop-Tart which rides on a rainbow. The application keeps track of the time you spend nyanning.

== Un-nyanning ==
To stop nyanning, press the Start button on your device.
